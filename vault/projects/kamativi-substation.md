Substation and switchgear construction for the 6MW power plant of the Kamativi Lithium Project. I am one of the design team engineers, contributing heavily to the electrical designs.

- Design of substation layouts, panel arrangements and equipment specifications.
- Installation and commissioning of MV/LV switchgear, distribution boards and power transformers.
- Panel fabrication oversight — from steel frame assembly to fully wired switchboards.
